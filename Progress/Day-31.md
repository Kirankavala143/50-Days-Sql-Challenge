## 📝 Queries Implemented

### 1. Find Employees with Total Salary Greater Than 100000
Calculated the total salary earned by each employee and filtered employees whose total salary exceeded 100000.

### 2. Show Employee Salary Along with Department Average Salary
Created a CTE to calculate department-wise average salary and joined it with employee salary details for comparison.

### 3. Find Department with Highest Total Salary
Calculated the total salary for each department and identified the department with the highest salary expenditure.

## 💡 Key Takeaway
CTEs simplify complex SQL queries by dividing them into logical steps. Combining CTEs with JOINs and aggregate functions makes analytical queries more readable, reusable, and efficient for real-world business reporting.
