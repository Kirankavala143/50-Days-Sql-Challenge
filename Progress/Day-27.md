## 📝 Queries Implemented

### 1. Compare Employee Salary with Overall Average Salary
Classified employees as:
- Above Average
- Below Average
- Equal

### 2. Compare Employee Salary with Total Company Salary
Identified:
- High Contributors (>10% of total salary)
- Low Contributors

### 3. Compare Department Salary with Overall Salary
Calculated department total salary using Window Functions and compared it with the company's total salary to identify:
- High Salary Departments
- Low Salary Departments

## 🚀 Concepts Learned
- Window Functions
- Aggregate Window Functions
- PARTITION BY
- OVER()
- CASE Statements
- Business Reporting using SQL
- Salary Analysis

## 💡 Key Takeaway
Window Functions allow us to compare individual rows with overall business metrics without collapsing data using GROUP BY. This makes them extremely powerful for analytical reporting.
