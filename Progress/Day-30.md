## 📝 Queries Implemented

### 1. Create Employee Salary CTE
Created a CTE by joining employee and salary tables to simplify salary analysis.

### 2. Calculate Department Average Salary
Computed the average salary for each department using aggregate functions inside a CTE.

### 3. Find Employees Earning Above Department Average
Compared employee salaries with their department's average salary using multiple CTEs.

### 4. Filter High-Paid Employees
Retrieved employees whose salary is greater than their department's average salary.

- WITH Clause

## 💡 Key Takeaway
CTEs improve query readability and make complex SQL logic easier to write, understand, and maintain. They are especially useful for breaking large analytical queries into smaller, reusable steps.
