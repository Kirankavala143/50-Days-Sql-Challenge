# Day 14: SQL Correlated Subqueries

## Tasks Completed

1. Find employees earning more than their department average salary.
2. Find employees with the highest salary in each department.
3. Find employees with the lowest salary in each department.

## Concepts Practiced

- Correlated Subqueries
- Aggregate Functions (AVG, MAX, MIN)
- Joins
- Department-wise Salary Analysis
- Employee Performance Reporting

## Key Learning

Correlated subqueries execute once for each row of the outer query. They help compare individual records with group-level metrics such as department averages, maximum salaries, and minimum salaries.
