# Day 13 - SQL Challenge

## Tasks Completed

1. Find employees earning more than the average salary
2. Find employees with the maximum salary
3. Find employees earning less than the average salary
4. Find employees with the minimum salary

## Concepts Practiced

- SQL Subqueries
- Aggregate Functions
  - AVG()
  - MAX()
  - MIN()
- Filtering with WHERE Clause
- Business Data Analysis

## Key Learning

Subqueries allow us to compare individual records against aggregated values such as average, maximum, and minimum salary. This helps in analyzing employee salary distribution and identifying patterns within organizational data.
