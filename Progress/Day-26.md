## Tasks Completed

### 1. Department-wise Employee Ranking
Used `DENSE_RANK()` to rank employees by salary within each department.

### 2. Salary Comparison
Compared every employee's salary with the average salary of their department using `AVG() OVER()` and `CASE`.

### 3. Top 3 Highest Paid Employees
Retrieved the three highest-paid employees from each department.

### 4. Lowest Paid Employee
Identified the employee with the lowest salary in every department.
