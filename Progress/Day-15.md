# Day 15 - SQL Challenge

## Topics Covered
- EXISTS
- NOT EXISTS
- Correlated Subqueries
- Employee Data Validation
- Data Integrity Checks

## Queries Solved

### 1. Employees With Salary Records
Used EXISTS to identify employees who have salary information.

### 2. Employees Without Salary Records
Used NOT EXISTS to find missing salary records.

### 3. Employees With Attendance Records
Retrieved employees having attendance entries.

### 4. Employees Without Attendance Records
Identified employees missing attendance records.

## Practice Tasks
- Employees With Performance Records
- Employees Without Performance Records
- Departments With Employees
- Departments Without Employees
- Employees With Salary And Attendance Records

## Key Learnings
- EXISTS improves readability for existence checks.
- NOT EXISTS helps identify missing relationships.
- Correlated subqueries are useful for validation and auditing.
- Commonly used in Data Analytics and Data Engineering projects.
