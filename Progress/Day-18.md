# Day 18 - SQL Practice Challenge

## Queries Solved

### 1. Employees with Average Performance Rating Greater Than 4

Calculated the average performance rating using ratings from 2022, 2023, and 2024 and filtered employees whose average rating exceeded 4.

### 2. Employees with More Than 10 Present Days

Used attendance records to identify employees with more than 10 days marked as Present.

### 3. Departments with Total Salary Paid Greater Than 200000

Aggregated salary data department-wise and filtered departments where total salary paid exceeded 200000.

### 4. Employees Earning More Than Their Department Average

Used a correlated subquery to compare each employee's salary against the average salary of their respective department.
