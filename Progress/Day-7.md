Day 7: Data Cleaning (Date Format Standardization & Validation)

Objective
Identify, validate, and standardize inconsistent date formats across multiple datasets to ensure data accuracy and consistency.

Tasks Completed

Analyzed date-related columns (hire_date, salary_date, attendance_date) for formatting inconsistencies.
Detected invalid date values such as incorrect months, invalid days, and malformed date patterns.
Converted dates from multiple formats into a standardized YYYY-MM-DD format.
Replaced invalid or unusable date values with NULL for further processing.
Updated missing and invalid dates using predefined business rules.
Ensured consistency and compatibility of date fields for analytics and reporting.

Key Learnings

Date quality issues can significantly impact reporting and analysis.
Standardized formats improve data reliability and system compatibility.
Data validation should be performed before transformation.
Cleaning date columns requires both technical checks and business context.

Outcome
Successfully standardized and validated date-related columns, improving overall dataset quality and ensuring readiness for downstream analytics.
