# Day 19 - SQL CASE WHEN Categorization

## Tasks Completed

### 1. Salary Category
Categorized employees based on salary ranges:
- Low (< 30000)
- Medium (30000 - 60000)
- High (> 60000)

### 2. Performance Category
Categorized employees using average performance ratings:
- Good (>= 4)
- Average (>= 3)
- Poor (< 3)

### 3. Attendance Category
Categorized attendance records:
- Active (Present)
- Inactive (Absent)

### 4. Experience Category
Categorized employees based on years of experience:
- Freshers (< 2 years)
- Mid-Level (2 - 5 years)
- Experienced (> 5 years)

## Concepts Practiced
- CASE WHEN
- Conditional Logic
- Data Categorization
- Business Reporting
- SQL Data Analysis

## Outcome
Learned how CASE WHEN can transform raw numerical data into meaningful business categories, making reports easier to understand and analyze.
